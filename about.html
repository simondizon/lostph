About Lost Ph

What is Lost PH?

LostPh is a website where you can search for missing people. From the word "lost" it corresponds to the function or use of the website which is for searching missing people and we called it LostPh because this system helps to search for missing people here in our country. Not just for children but also for those old people who do not have the capability to go home or remember their way home resulting for them to become missing. Their relatives or families can visit the website to search for them. In this way, they can easily find that missing person.

Why Lost PH?

Lost PH is very useful. This website can help you in finding missing people in an easier way and advanced way because you are using the technology to search for someone. In this way you can also experience the convenience that technology can bring to you. This is not just helpful for searching people but also for investigations and studies done by authorities and researchers.
